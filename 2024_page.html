"2024 Most Anticipated Game"

Winner of the 2023 Game Award for "2024 Most Anticipated Game", Final Fantasy VII Rebirth is the highly anticipated new story in the remake project; a reimagining of the iconic original game into three standalone title by its original creators.

Expect a new standard of cinematic storytelling, fast paced combat and rich expoloration across a vast world. Unravel a gripping narrative - witness the personal journey of each party member and strengthen their bonds to work togeher and face off against powerful enemies in this unforgettable adventure.
