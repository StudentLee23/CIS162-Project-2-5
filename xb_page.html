Xbox's "MUST PLAY" Games of 2023

  • Hi-Fi Rush is a nice surprise from Tango Gameworks. It has a lot of charm and a lot of heart. With its awesome combat and platforming, vivid visual design, and unwavering commitment to comedy, Hi-Fi Rush is an undenialby wild ride.

  • Forza Motorsport is finally here and it cement itself as one of the best-looking and most approachable racing simulators to date. It is one of the best acccessible surprises in 2023. Forza Motorsport is an exhilirating racing experience that seamlessly combines stunning visuals and realstic gameplay.

  • The long-awaited spacefaring RPG from the team behind The Elder Scrolls and Fallout is undoubtedly a massive game. The world is expansive, and the main story and all the side-quests are brilliantly crafted. Starfield is an almost endless adventure that leads to more discovering of new details years down the line.
